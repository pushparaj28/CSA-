Computer Software Application & Training 2025-2026 Batch

The Computer Software Application (CSA) 2nd Shift batch of 2025-2026 represents a group of dedicated trainers mastering modern IT paradigms. This batch is characterized by its rigorous practical training and a commitment to technical excellence.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. Through guided mentorship and hands-on laboratory sessions, we empower our trainees to become future-ready professionals.

Why Choose CSA 2ND SHIFT

CSA 2nd Shift is more than just a classroom — it feels like a family where everyone supports and helps each other grow. Our teachers always motivate students, guide us in every step, and give everyone equal opportunities to learn and work on practical tasks. The environment is friendly, positive, and full of teamwork, which makes learning more enjoyable and comfortable. Along with studies, students also get enough flexibility to manage their personal work and improve their skills without pressure. In CSA 2nd Shift, we don’t just learn technology — we build confidence, creativity, and strong connections together. 🚀
