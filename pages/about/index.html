About of CSA 2ND Shift

IToT Lucknow is a key center for the Craft Instructor Training Scheme (CITS). Located in the heart of Lucknow at Aliganj, it serves as a hub for aspiring instructors who wish to master their trades and pedagogy.

We specialize in providing high-quality vocational training, especially in the Computer Software Applications (CSA) trade, ensuring that our trainees are equipped with the latest industry standards and teaching methodologies.

CITS Excellence

The Craft Instructor Training Scheme (CITS) at IToT Lucknow is designed to train future instructors for ITIs across India, focusing on both technical proficiency and teaching skills.

Advanced CSA Trade

Our Computer Software Application trade covers everything from database management and networking to advanced web development and software engineering principles.

Industrial Alignment

We work closely with industry standards to ensure our curriculum remains relevant, providing our students with the skills needed to lead in the digital era.

2nd Shift Community

The 2nd Shift at IToT Lucknow is a vibrant community of proactive learners. Our schedule allows flexibility for those balancing other commitments while receiving intensive vocational training.

Campus & Infrastructure

The IToT Lucknow campus at Aliganj is equipped with modern computer labs, high-speed connectivity, and smart classrooms. Our infrastructure supports an intensive learning environment where theory meets practical implementation.

Being part of the Government's skill development ecosystem, we provide a structured and professional atmosphere that fosters growth, discipline, and technical innovation.

With a focus on creating the next generation of expert trainers, IToT Lucknow remains a cornerstone of vocational education and technical excellence in Uttar Pradesh.
